About me

Am Sheku Nyallay currently a third year student pursuing a Bachelor of science(BSc) in Computer Science at the University of Makeni(UNIMAK). Early in 2013 I gained an admission at the Al-Mustapha university(An affiliated university in Iran) situated at 58 Wilkinson road Freetown, where I sat to the National Council for TechnicalVocational and other Academic Awards(NCTVA)pursued and achieved in Higher National Diploma(HND) in Modern Languages with Division one result 2016. Because of my resilience in education I also gained an admission in Imam Khomeini Relief Foundation Sierra Leone Chapter,and certified of Achieving a Diploma in Computer Studies. Meanwhile, as a motivational youth with credible vision, I also certified of Achieving in Practical Digital Training Courses on Website Designing,Blogging and Digital Marketing at Youth for peace and Security African Sierra Leone Network (YPS)on the 12 of August 2019. As a youth inspiring for National development, Network is a most important aspect for the development of society,I also certified for successful completion of two days training on impactful volunteerism,and how to be volunteers impactful as a Youth for Nation building. Furthermore,as my Aims of achieving sustainable peace and development in Sierra Leone, I was also recognised in National Youth Conference on peace and development and certified as a National Peace Ambassador,under youth for peace and Security African(YPS). And also, I participated and certified for three days Youth capacity Building Training with the themes of implementing Skills training for youths Empowerment and sustainable development in Nation building,under Progressive Youth Organisation Sierra Leone. Furthermore I was also recognised and certified in two days training on Youth in Agriculture under Purpose for Living Sierra Leone. Because of my dedication and commitment with my colleagues students am currently serving in the capacity of their Finance Secretary in our Noble department.
